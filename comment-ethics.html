Socials

(i:) We want you to follow certain Rules when commenting!

0 - Follow local Rules!

For Youtube, this would be the Youtube Community Guidlines,
but not only the Rules of the Platform, but also the Rules of the Community!
For the r/ethernia Subreddit, this would be the Subreddit Rules.

1 - Be nice!

No discrimination, No toxicity, etc.

2 - No Spam, No Copypaste!

Don't use copypastas as comments and don't just copy others comments!
And of yourse, don't spam!

3 - Is this a comment?

Your comment has to be an actual comment.
No "hi" or "first"!

4 - Stay on topic!

Don't have a private conversation with someone!
You got Direct Messages or Chats for that!

5 - No Self-Advertisement

Self-Advertisements are not allowed, please ask the Staff Team first!

6 - No Spoilers!

or if possible, hide Spoilers!
This is what a hidden Spoiler looks like on Discord:

|| spoiler goes here ||

7 - Dual-Questions

If someone already asked a question, don't repeat it!
If a question was already answered, don't repeat it!

8 - Moderatable Comments

We have English and German Moderators, for some Places this is not a problem,
but please try to keep the comments english or german, so we can moderate them!

9 - No wrong Facts!

We don't want lies, we only want facts.
You can ask about something, but don't spread fake news!

10 - No NSFW Content!

NSFW (and NSFL) Content is only allowed, if the Staff Team permits,
or it is in a seperate NSFW Channel or marked as NSFW with a Spoiler.

11 - Don't share private Data!

If you aren't allowed to share private Data, this will not only be deleted, but also prosecuted!

12 - Common Sense

Just don't be an idiot!
